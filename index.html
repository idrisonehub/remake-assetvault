Our story

Dave Southen, a seasoned professional in the construction industry, recognised a pressing issue that was costing both time and resources on job sites: the theft of valuable tradie tools. In the high-stakes world of construction, where every minute and every tool counts, this challenge demanded an innovative solution.

Dave knew that there had to be an easier way to keep track of these valuable assets. Asset Vault became the answer. His commitment to enhancing efficiency and security on construction sites made Asset Vault the trusted solution for professionals in the field.

Asset Vault is now extending its functionality to customers outside the construction sector, revolutionising the way we manage our personal assets and streamline home admin tasks.
